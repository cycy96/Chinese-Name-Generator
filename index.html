About Chinese Name

In Chinese names, the surname (xìngshì; 姓氏) typically consists of a single character and is placed before the given name (míng; 名).
The given name is usually composed of two characters and is disyllabic.

Chinese Names: Surname (xìngshì; 姓氏) + Given Name (míng; 名)
Example: 陈 (xìngshì; 姓氏) + 秀丽 (míng; 名)
